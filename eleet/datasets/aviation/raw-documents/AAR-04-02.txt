The flight was operating under the provisions of 14 Code of Federal Regulations Part 121 as a scheduled cargo flight from Memphis International Airport, in Memphis, Tennessee, to TLH. Night visual meteorological conditions prevailed for the flight, which operated on an instrument flight rules flight plan.
